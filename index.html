The struggle is real.

(And you are definitely not alone)

"Running is too hard."

It is when you try to sprint on day one. We remove the pressure and focus on gentle, manageable intervals that actually feel good.

"I run out of breath."

Most beginners run way too fast. We'll teach you the "conversational pace" that lets you build stamina without gasping for air.

"I'm intimidated."

This isn't an elite track club. It's a supportive, private community of normal people cheering each other on every step of the way.

How the 30 days break down

A structured, gentle progression designed specifically to build your physical and mental confidence.

Week 1

Getting Moving

Mostly walking, with tiny 60-second jogs sprinkled in. The goal is simply to build the habit of getting out the door.

15 mins / 3x a week
Week 2

Finding Your Rhythm

We shift to 50/50 walk and jog splits. Your body starts adapting, and you'll notice your breathing getting easier.

20 mins / 3x a week
Week 3

Pushing the Pace

Longer jogs, shorter walks. This is the magic week where you realize you are actually capable of doing this.

25 mins / 3x a week
Week 4

The Continuous Run

You will surprise yourself. By the end of this week, you'll complete a continuous run without stopping to walk.

30 mins / 3x a week
