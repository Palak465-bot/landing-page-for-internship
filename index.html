Enter: The Run Club Method

We slow it down. Way down. Here is exactly what happens over 30 days.

1

Week 1: Find Your Feet

Mostly walking. We mix in tiny 60-second jogs just to get your heart saying hello. No pressure, just movement.

2

Week 2: The Rhythm

Equal parts walking and jogging. You'll start noticing that you aren't completely out of breath anymore.

3

Week 3: The Tipping Point

More jogging, less walking. This is the magical week where your body actually starts to crave the run.

4

Week 4: Unstoppable

You will run continuously for 20-30 minutes. You will surprise yourself. You are officially a runner.
